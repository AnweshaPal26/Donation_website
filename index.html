Children's Upliftment

Children are the rocks on which society's future will be built,they are our greatest asset as a nation and the leaders of tomorrow. Y4D believes Every child in the world is entitled to get good health, quality education and a clean environment to grow. Y4D engages with children from stages of infancy, childhood to adolescence through focused interventions on their Health, nutrition, education and socio-emotional development. Y4D enables well nurtured children with full opportunities for growth in a safe and protected environment, through its various initiatives in the following areas:
Health and Nutrition
Education
Socio-Emotional and Life Skills development
